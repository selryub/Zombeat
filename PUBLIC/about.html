OUR STORY

FCSIT Kiosk, operated by PERTEKMA (student organization of FCSIT-UNIMAS), is a food and beverages kiosk located at Block B, Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (FCSIT) at University Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS). Provide quick and affordable meals designed to meet the needs of students, lecturers, and staff with busy schedules.

FCSIT Kiosk Website is a web system that people can use to buy products from the kiosk. Students and staff can purchase a product by registering and logging into the system.
